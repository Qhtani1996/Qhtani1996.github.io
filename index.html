About Me

Passionate about using data to improve public health outcomes

Hello, I'm Meshal!

As a Master of Public Health professional with expertise in epidemiology and biostatistics, I specialize in analyzing complex health datasets to uncover meaningful patterns and trends that inform evidence-based decision-making.

My work focuses on leveraging statistical programming languages like R and Stata to conduct rigorous analyses, create interactive visualizations, and develop comprehensive health reports that bridge the gap between data and actionable public health strategies.

With experience in mortality analysis, disease surveillance, and health systems research, I'm committed to using data science to address pressing public health challenges in the GCC region and beyond.
